Right, here we are then. I have finally set up a place for writing down my thoughts.

Who am I to be writing about software? No one really, just a lucky software developer working in Europe. I have 5 years of experience. Not all of my career has been working as a developer. I spent 6 years working in various non tech roles before returning to education and then getting a job in development, which I have enjoyed, albeit without much financial success. I believe that my experience gives me some credibility to comment on tech, which I will do so on this website. Among other topics.

I develop communications software for a medium sized corporation. Working mostly with Java, deploying to an on prem hosted Kubernetes platform. It’s been interesting.

I will be writing about working in tech on this website. Including languages and platforms, dealing with other people, customers, or anything else I think is useful to write about.

This static website is hosted on Linode and served by an Nginx web server. You can see how easy that is to do from the Linode guide: https://www.linode.com/docs/guides/set-up-web-server-host-website/

This website is intended to check a box - one that has been in my head for years; To blog about tech from time to time. I’m hoping that it will help me grow, that is the idea.

Thank you for visiting this site.
